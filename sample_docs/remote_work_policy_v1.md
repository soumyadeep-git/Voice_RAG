# Acme Corp Remote Work Policy (2023)

## Office Attendance
All employees must work from the office at least 3 days per week. Remote work is permitted for the remaining days with manager approval.

## Core Hours
Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time. Employees are expected to be available during these hours.

## Home Office Stipend
The company provides a home office stipend of $200 per year to cover equipment and internet costs.

## Equipment
Laptops are provided by the company. Employees are responsible for their own desk and chair.
