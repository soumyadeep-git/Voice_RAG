# Acme Corp Information Security Policy

## Passwords
Passwords must be at least 12 characters and rotated every 90 days. Reusing any of the last 5 passwords is not allowed.

## Multi-Factor Authentication
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all company systems, including email and the VPN.

## Device Security
All company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT within 24 hours.

## Incident Reporting
Suspected security incidents must be reported to the security team within 24 hours of discovery.
