# Acme Corp Remote Work Policy (2024)

## Office Attendance
All employees must work from the office at least 2 days per week. The remaining days may be worked remotely without prior approval.

## Core Hours
Core collaboration hours are 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time. Meetings should be scheduled within this window.

## Home Office Stipend
The company provides a home office stipend of $500 per year, reimbursed quarterly, to cover equipment and internet costs.

## Equipment
Laptops are provided by the company. Employees may expense a desk and chair up to $300.
