# Acme Corp Benefits Overview

## Health Insurance
The company covers 80% of the health insurance premium for employees and 50% for dependents.

## Paid Time Off
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, in addition to public holidays.

## Retirement
The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 4% of base salary.

## Parental Leave
Eligible employees receive 12 weeks of paid parental leave.
